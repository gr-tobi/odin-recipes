Have a Pizza This!

A Classic Meaty Pie

What's better than slabs of meat on some saucy dough? Nothing! Great as a treat, great as a hangover cure, great straight out the fridge, it truly doesn't matter. Serves 3-6 people.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1/4 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 1 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil if needed
  • 1/3-1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 3 tablespoon parmesan cheese
  • 3-4 slices bacon cooked and chopped
  • 1/2 cup ham roughly chopped
  • 1/2 cup hot sausage cooked and crumbled
  • 1/4 cup pepperoni slices
  • Ground black pepper

Cooking Steps

  1. Prepare dough by combining warm water, heated to 115 degrees F, with yeast and sugar. Stir to dissolve and allow to proof.
  2. In the bowl of a stand mixer, add flour and salt. With mixer running on low, slowly add yeast mixture. Increase speed, adding olive oil, if needed or desired. Mix until a ball forms, pulling away from the sides of the bowl. Transfer to a greased bowl. Cover and allow to rise in a warm location until doubled, roughly 1 hour.
  3. Meanwhile, cook bacon and sausage. Chop bacon and ham. Crumble sausage.
  4. Preheat overn to 425 degrees F.
  5. Grease a baking sheet with olive oil. Shape dough to desired size. Spoon pizza sauce over dough. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese and 1/2 cup shredded whole milk mozzarella. Add meat toppings. Finally sprinkle with remaining 1 cup mozzrella and ground black pepper.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until desired crispness is reached. Serve warm.
